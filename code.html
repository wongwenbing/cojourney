Digitalisation

As part of the school's digitalisation processes, every student had an access to an iPad, a Microsoft Teams account. Furthermore, there was a need to reach out to new Secondary One students as it was observed that there was a decreasing trends.

Creation of the CCA Microsoft Teams

There were multiple reasons for creating the Microsoft Teams as listed below.



  1. Increasing further communication between members and the committee. Through the Microsoft Teams, students are able to directly message any member of the committee without going through the Section Leader, who would then have to relay the message with the relevant committee members.


  2. Allowing members to access relevant information. The benefit is that students are able to access CCA schedules, CCA seating plans and many other information.
  3. Members are able to collaborate for relevant projects.

    Members are able to communicate through Microsoft Teams and collaborate such as discussing their musical ways, or even collaborating to discuss how the CCA can be made more inclusive.

Digitalisation of CCA Processes

I often found many of the CCA processes too rigid and that some of these processes could be digitalised.
CCA Attendance

A problem that I noticed was that our secretaries had difficulties identifying who was present. The orginal attendance was arranged by name, and hence difficult to identify by sections. Furthermore, it is printed and hence it does not prove to be environmentally-friendly and would be troublesome as we need to wait for our CCA teacher to print out the sheets.

After the change in systems, the secretaries have feedbacked to us that they found it much more convenient for them.

Submissions of Parent Letters and Medical Certificates (MC)


During COVID-19, many members were unable to submit their parent letters and MCs to us for it to be keyed into the school system. As such, we developed the form such that the submissions could reach us prompty and that the school system would be able to show a VR sign as soon as possible.
